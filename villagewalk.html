Village walk, Kumaon – A move in the right direction

“If you want to see the modernization move to city but if you want to see the culture move to village” Get ready for the thrilling trip of Himalayan village walk to explore the alluring exquisiteness and to witness the picturesque Himalayan hamlet, marvelous snow - covered towering peaks and relish the cultural hospitality of mountainous region. Peacefully listen to the chirping of birds, the rustling wind and the tinkling on the bells from the cattle herds and of course you will get the opportunity to mingle with the local villager to know more about the region and its culture. In this spectacular Himalayan foothill area captivate yourself with charm of the sky creeping trees of virgin pine, oak and many more, appealing terraced fields, and enticing flora and fauna during the tender walk between village to village in the Himalayan foothill areas.Your stay during the village walk will let you taste the delicious mountainous cuisines and traditional homestays to have a perfect experience of culture and congenial warm welcome by the local communities of the region.These heritage homestays are well furnished to facilitate you with the best accommodation. Despite their traditional looksthese homestays are well - versed with all the modern equipment to convert it into a cozy cottage for your comfortable stay without sacrificing the urban comfort. The best part is the option to customize your trip. Yes!We know that your interest may vary from others hence we avail the option to make a customize trips by keeping all the villages as per your priority list and also increase or decrease the halt duration as per your capabilities.

During the entire village walk trip you will get to stay in the homestays that will you give a glimpse of the core and unobserved culture that you could typically visualize in the villages at the foothill villages of the Himalaya. You will feel contented and pleased with the hospitality and amiability of the local community. They will tell you the region folk tales and other information related to the place and its history. Despite their traditional looks that is totally influenced with the culture of the region, you will discover the homestays entirely exotic, cozy and well-furnished from inside. Though the homestays are nestled around the dense forest of alpine and oak they are not less than a luxurious hotel room with all the essential amenities and services. While peeping out of the window you will see the excellent view of the valley, dense forests or terraced field laden with seasonal fruits.

Day 1 – (Destination-Deora) (Walk – 4-5 hours)

After having the breakfast, you will initiate your village walk for your first destination ‘Deora’. The entire path is surrounded with the exotic beauty of the Himalayan snow-capped peaks and the magnificent exquisiteness of the nature. You will go to Wintola village via Math village. At Math village you will get the chance to mingle with the natives of the region to know more about their tradition. You will be amazed to see their friendliness and the description of their culture. Meanwhile you will see an enticing water fall “The Balta Fall”. Surrounded with the natural majesties and amusing birdsongs the Balta fall is an ultimate beautiful spot where you will have a tea break and bit relaxation at the peaceful spot.

Deora to Doulchina (Walk 5 hours)

On the very next morning, after waving your bye to Deora's house, you will depart for Dhoulchina. We will cross 'purani ghat' during the route to Paleo village. You will be stunned to get the squints of wondrous natural beauty. Here you will enjoy a tea break while paddling in the trifles of shallow water of canals. You will stay the night at Daulchina village.

Daulchina to BudJageshwer/Jageshwer

We will begin the bad with a tangy, titbit breakfast followed by our walk towards budjogeshwer points. Budjogeshwer point is a stunning viewpoint that gives a 360-degree view of the Himalayas. This would be an inevitable view of the Himalayan landscape that is out of one's imagination. Further, we will visit the Budjogeshwer and proceed towards our guesthouse to rest and halt.

Budh Jageshwer to Jageshwer (4-5 hours walk)

After taking breakfast, we will proceed toward Budjogeshwer temple. On the way, you will enjoy the marvelous beauty of the Himalayas. Further, you will enter the dense forests and woods leading to Jogeshwer. Here you will visit the famous Jogeshwer temple. You will stay in Jogeshwer at night.

We will step back to Kasar and will wave your bye to Almora, and you will lead towards your destination.
